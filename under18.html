Under 18s

AT 9 DEGREES WE CATER PREDOMINANTLY FOR THOSE 16 AND OVER.

Although 9 Degrees endeavours to be an inclusive facility we are first and foremost an adult venue with climbing most suitable for those 5ft tall and above.

If you want to go rock climbing with your child, but you're not sure if 9 Degrees is a good choice, feel free to call us. We can help with your questions or suggest other kid-friendly rock climbing gyms nearby. It's important to have supervision for young climbers, and if you're in a big group or plan to leave the kids alone, it may be better to explore other suitable forms of entertainment.

  • 1.

    • UNDER 18s MUST HAVE THEIR WAIVER SIGNED BY A PARENT OR LEGAL GUARDIAN

    Siblings, parents of friends or other non-legal guardians will not be eligible to sign a waiver for a child. Signed notes, emails or verbal permission will not be accepted.

  • 2.

    • KIDS UNDER 16 NEED ADULT SUPERVISION AT ALL TIMES.

    Although bouldering does not require an adult to belay, active supervision is required at all times. Supervisors must not let children run, sit or stand on the mats, climb too close to anyone else, jump down from the top of the wall or otherwise act in an unsafe or disruptive manner.

  • 3.

    • CLIMBING SHOES MUST BE WORN AT ALL TIMES

    At 9 Degrees, climbers must wear climbing shoes while on the wall. Rent shoes for $7 or free on the first visit if needed. We mainly serve customers 16 and older, offering rental shoes from US size 3.5/women's size 5 or EU size 35. For small child feet, guests must bring their own. Your understanding in keeping the gym safe and clean is appreciated.

  • 4.

    • FOR EVERY 2 CLIMBING CHILDREN, THERE MUST BE ONE SUPERVISING ADULT.

    A group of 4 children would require 2 adults, for example. This is non-negotiable. Supervision exemptions may be made by staff on a case-by-case basis for competent youth competition climbers.

  • 5.

    • IF YOU HAVE 5 OR MORE CHILDREN OR PLAN TO LEAVE KIDS UNATTENDED, CONSIDER DIFFERENT ENTERTAINMENT OPTIONS.

    If you have any unanswered questions please don't hesitate to ask.
