Parking Info
    Follow this link to find us via Google Maps. Turn off South St and park in bays marked 'unit 11' near our main entrance. More parking is available along South St and in the (free) Council parking lot in the park.

Public Transport
    Bicycle - Use the excellent bicycle path along Parramatta River.

    Ferry - Rydalmere wharf is 600 m from our front door and provides a great way to get to anywhere along the Parramatta river.

    Train - The closest is Rydalmere station (18 min walk) but trains are more frequent from Parramatta train station.
