Parking Info
    Car parking in Alexandria is a complex issue. Please ask our staff for all the tricks and tips for parking nearby or call us on 0408 870 473.
    PLEASE do not park in our neighbours allocated spots.

    WEEKDAYS Until 6pm: Use any Pay & Display area at Sydney Corporate Park at $3/hr. For more information see http://www.sydneycorporatepark.com/parking/

    WEEKENDS Until 6pm: Free parking for 2 hrs in any Pay & Display area at Sydney Corporate Park (no ticket required), afterwards $3/hr and $7/day.
