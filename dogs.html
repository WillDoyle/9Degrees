Dogs

WE LOVE OUR FURRY FRIENDS BUT OUR CUSTOMERS WILL ALWAYS BE OUR FIRST PRIORITY


Your dog is very welcome to visit all 9 Degrees gyms. We ask that dogs are kept on leash for the duration of your session and do not become disruptive or overly excited. Although we love dogs the climbing gym is a space for them to curl up and nap, not play.


We reserve the right to ask any dogs to leave at any time if they cannot behave suitably.

Dog Owner House Rules

  • Dogs must always enter the gym on a lead
  • Do not bring a young puppy who is not yet house trained
  • Chew toys are great but please leave excitable toys like balls at home. Although there is plenty of space, we are not a park.
  • Please do not use the gym as a space for dog socialisation or play. Dog owners should do their best to keep space between dogs. Play is not permitted.
  • Dogs are not allowed on the mats under any circumstances
