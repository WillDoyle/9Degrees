What is Bouldering?

Bouldering is rock climbing without the ropes, heights, and hassle. It’s physical problem solving. Distinctly simple, bouldering is one of the fastest growing sports in the world. It’s a highly social form of climbing that is easy to learn and enjoy.

Membership Options

CASUAL ENTRY

$24

Casual entry grants you entry to climb for as long as you like on a single day. Shoe hire is free on your first visit or an extra $7 subsequently. $22 student entry available with a valid ID.

TWO WEEK TRIAL

$49

For just $49 you’ll have unlimted access to all 9 Degrees gyms for a two week period. An introductory offer only available to anyone over the age of 16 who haven’t previously held a membership or 10 visit pass.

10 VISIT PASS

$216

With a 2 year expiry and the ability to use it at all 9 Degrees gyms a 10 visit pass is a flexible option that is perfect for anybody visiting regularly but not quite often enough to need a membership.

PREPAID MEMBERSHIPS


Memberships are the best option for those visiting at least twice a week. Enjoy unlimited access to all gyms, retail discounts and the ability to pause your membership for as long as you need at no cost.
